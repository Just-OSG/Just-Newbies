Just Newbies is an educational platform created for university students getting started in the IT field. The goal is to provide clear, beginner-friendly explanations of fundamental concepts and technologies across various IT domains.

This project is part of Just OSG (Jordan University of Science and Technology Open Source Group), a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting open-source culture and providing free educational resources to students.

This project was inspired by [Algorithmica](https://en.algorithmica.org/) and uses their open-source Hugo theme (MIT License). Original theme created by Sergey Slotin.


If you found an error or want to contribute? Visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/just-osg/Just-Newbies).
