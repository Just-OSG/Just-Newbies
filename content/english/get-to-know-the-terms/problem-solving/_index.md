Problem solving is at the heart of programming and computer science. It's the process of breaking down complex challenges into smaller, manageable parts and finding logical solutions. Developing strong problem-solving skills and computational thinking will help you tackle any programming challenge, design efficient algorithms, and become a better developer overall.

**Topics:** Computational thinking, Breaking down problems, Algorithm design, Pseudocode, Debugging strategies
