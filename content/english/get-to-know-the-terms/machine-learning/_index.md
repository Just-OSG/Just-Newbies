Machine Learning is a branch of artificial intelligence that enables computers to learn from data and make decisions without being explicitly programmed. Instead of writing specific rules, we train models on data so they can recognize patterns and make predictions. ML powers many technologies we use daily, from recommendation systems to voice assistants, and is one of the most exciting fields in modern technology.
